Launch of new health insurance AI tool could help hospitals conserve billions

By Maya Benjamin

Waystar's existing services have helped patients simplify health-care payments, but Waystar's new generative AI tool could help lighten a heavy and costly load for hospitals: contesting insurance denials.

Source: 2024 Nasdaq, Inc. / Vanja Savi

Waystar's new technology, named AltitudeCare, creates appeal letters using generative AI. Fighting denied insurance claims costs hospitals and health systems almost $20 billion annually, according to CNBC. Therefore, Waystar claims Altitude Care could free providers of this tedious work and significantly reduce costs.

“We think if we can develop software that makes people’s lives better in an otherwise stressful moment of time when they’re getting health care, then we’re doing something good.”

—Matt Hawkins, Waystar CEO, CNBC
